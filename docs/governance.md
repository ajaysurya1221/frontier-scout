# Governance

Frontier Scout maps to the NIST AI RMF functions:

- **Govern:** ADRs, prompt registry metadata, policy defaults, and release
  gates define ownership and review.
- **Map:** reference stack, architecture docs, and threat model describe
  context and risks.
- **Measure:** eval harness, coverage gates, spans, audit logs, and CI provide
  measurable quality signals.
- **Manage:** ReBAC, approval interrupts, cost budgets, and policy guards reduce
  operational risk.
